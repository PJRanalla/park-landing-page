About City Park

City Park is Park City's oldest and largest park. Located just a few blocks from City Center, it's our city's premier spot for all outdoor activities. We invite you come and explore all the amenities our park has to offer. Enjoy a picnic on The City Green. Bike, walk, or run on our many walkpaths throughout the park. Nestle yourself under the perfect tree and open one of your favorite books. Whatever you decide, we hope you decide to do it in City Park.
